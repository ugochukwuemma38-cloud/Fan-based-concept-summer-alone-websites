ABOUT

Hailing from North-West London, Summer Alone is a complete enigma in every sense of the word. Growing up in a household where he was surrounded by multiple genres heavily influenced his sound today. Music was always his passion and after beginning to teach himself to produce, he later on decided it was finally time to jump on the mic himself. After spending years finding his sound and perfecting his craft, Summer Alone took a leap of faith on a journey to Los Angeles, California to link up with some of his friends online and try to further push his music career. It turned out to be one of the best decisions he’s made. He performed at multiple shows, met some of his idols who praised his talents and the audiences were enamoured by him. SUMMER isn’t content with being placed in a box; his latest album (at the time of writing) “Wallflower in Baphomet’s Garden” is a beautifully melancholic alternative journey through his eclectic thoughts. Summer Alone is inspired by the likes of $uicideboy$, Lil Peep, Yung Lean , BONES & XXXTENTACION. In his later work, there is an obvious not change but adaptation to his sound within production and genre such as a heavy inspiration from the DNB, Drill, Alt Rock & Grunge scene SUMMER ALONE channels their emotional introspection and openness in his own unique captivating way. Much of SUMMER ALONE’s music focuses on struggles with mental health, something he’s incredibly passionate about and hopes that he can highlight the importance mental health truly is to the youth of today.
